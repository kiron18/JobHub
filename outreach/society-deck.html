The system, step three

Stop asking.
Start offering.

The mistake everyone makes
"Please give me a job. Any openings? Please." It is the most common message hiring managers get, and the easiest to ignore.
Lead with value, not need
Show genuine interest. Show you understand their work. Show you would contribute. Be the bright spark they remember.
Every employer is doing maths
Hiring is a bet to minimise loss. Present yourself as the person who multiplies their return, and the conversation changes.

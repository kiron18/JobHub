Partnership Overview

Turning job-ready graduates into hired professionals
1 The gap we close

Technical training makes someone hire-ready. It doesn't make them hired. That last step — turning a qualified candidate into a person who actually lands the role — is its own discipline, and it's the one most programs can't deliver. That's the gap I fill.

I work with international migrants and career switchers to convert "job-ready" into "employed." The bottleneck is almost never the technical skill. It's positioning, communication and the application process itself. Fix those and the candidate you've already trained starts getting interviews.

Shift in how they present their value

The offer itself depends on factors no one can honestly guarantee — and I'm upfront about that. The deeper outcome is a permanent shift in how someone presents and communicates their value, which serves them well beyond this one job.

I don't sell job guarantees. The market is full of people who do, and most of them are selling false hope. I sell a system and a transformation — and I get results because of it.

3 How it works

A structured three-month coaching cohort: weekly sessions, ongoing access between calls, and proprietary tools my clients use to run a data-driven application process — tracking, follow-up systems, outreach and LinkedIn support.

Candidates set their own activity targets and we hold them to the work. Built on direct input from 100+ recruiters and hiring managers, and tested on real outcomes.

4 Why it fits you

Your program already produces skilled, confident graduates. Pairing that with a dedicated outcome layer closes your funnel end to end — training plus genuine job results — and lets you market a tangible outcome without overpromising.

I'd position this as an integrated premium outcome track, not a bolt-on, so it strengthens your offer rather than diluting it.

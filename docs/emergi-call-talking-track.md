# Emergi Partnership Call - Talking Track (private)

**Your stance:** Warm and collaborative. You want this. Say yes to starting, accept $700 for the first few cohorts, and quietly protect a handful of things that cost them nothing but matter to you. Volume now means wins now, and wins are your real leverage later.

---

## Your banner (settle this in your head first)
- You run under **Aussie Grad Careers**. Emergi is a **partner and a candidate channel**, not a white-label buyer. Their grads flow into your program and your community.
- That is good for you: more people in the community means more peer momentum and more wins on the board, which is exactly the scaling program you want to build.
- Warm way to say it: "Candidates come through into the AGC program and community, so they get the full system and the group around them. You get placed candidates and a partner who owns the outcome layer for you."
- Only if they specifically want Emergi-branded delivery does this become a different (white-label) conversation, priced differently. Default is your banner.

## Opening frame (first 60 seconds)
"Before we talk numbers I want to show you what you are plugging into. Most people sell you a coach. I have built a system: the method, proprietary software that runs the whole job-landing process, and an accountability layer that tells us both, with no guessing, whether the work is happening. Let me show you." Then go to the demo.

## The money (keep it easy)
- **Accept $700 incl GST per head for the first 2 to 3 cohorts.** Some money plus a steady stream of candidates plus wins beats holding out. Do not haggle now.
- For your own head: $700 incl GST is about $636 ex GST. Fine as a starting rate.
- Plant one gentle seed, then leave it: "Let's run the first cohorts at this, see the results, and revisit the model once there is a track record and you are scaling to monthly."
- **For later rounds only, once wins exist or if they raise it**, the directions worth moving toward:
  - Higher per head ($900 to $1,200) as volume proves out.
  - Base plus placement bonus (e.g. $500 plus $500 to $700 on placement) so pay tracks outcomes.
  - Revenue share on a premium outcome tier they price to students.
- None of this goes on the shared deck. Keep it verbal, for later.

## Senior direct entrants (separate, do not settle today)
These are premium clients with real local experience, not $700 grads. Full-fee coaching at your own rate. If any fee moves, it is a referral cut to Emergi, not you taking $700. Flag it as its own conversation.

## Small things to lock (cheap for them, valuable to you)
- **"Passive support until placed" means community and async access, not open-ended 1:1.** Say it plainly so it is not an uncapped commitment.
- **Define "placed"** (any job, or a relevant data role).
- **You keep the right to use anonymised wins** as AGC testimonials and case studies.

## Questions to ask them
- Cohort size today versus the move to monthly, and when.
- **The juniors' local experience: how substantial is the guaranteed internship really?** Their proposal says grads get local experience via a 12-week internship. Worth confirming it is real work, because your placement odds lean on it. (Seniors clearly have it; the juniors are the open question.)
- Who owns the resume, given they already provide an ATS resume and cover letter. Position your workspace as the ongoing per-job engine, not a redo.
- Capacity they want you to commit per cohort.

## Close
"I'm in. Let's agree the scope, I'll send a short one-pager, and we run the first cohort." Confirm the single next step and end there.

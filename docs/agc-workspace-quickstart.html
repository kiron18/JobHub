Program Quickstart · Module 1

Your first application, live in 24 hours.

The workspace does the heavy lifting: it reads your resume once, then writes a tailored resume and cover letter for every job you go for. Your job is to feed it the truth and quality-check what comes out. Here is the whole loop.

  1. Upload your resume once

    The workspace builds your diagnostic and your Profile Bank: every role, project, qualification and achievement, structured.

  2. Fix your Profile Bank before anything else

    The bank is the source of truth for every document generated. Check each role, date and achievement. Add what your resume never mentioned: the uni project, the freelance work. That is often your strongest evidence.

  3. Set your target role and city

    Pick the role where what you want, what interests you, and what you have tangible evidence for overlap (your Venn worksheet from Lesson 1.2). This drives your job feed.

  4. Pick a job

    Use the matched jobs in your feed, ranked for early-career fit, or paste in any job you found yourself on SEEK or LinkedIn.

  5. Generate your documents

    Tailored resume and cover letter in about a minute. Applying for a government or university role? Generate the selection criteria responses too.

  6. Run the 60-second quality check

    You are the final gate. Never send anything unreviewed.

  7. Apply, and let the tracker take over

    Every application is logged automatically. The tracker tells you when to follow up, so nothing falls through the cracks.

  8. Post your first application in Gate Submissions

    Attach the resume you sent. Your coach spot-checks it on the next live call. That post completes Module 1.

The 60-second quality check

  • Every fact, date and employer is true, nothing invented, nothing inflated
  • The summary sounds like you and names the role you are applying for
  • Every bullet is an outcome achieved, not a task performed
  • Skills listed are skills you genuinely have and can speak to in an interview
Your daily rhythm
(45 to 90 min)
  1. Apply Hit today's application number through the workspace
  2. Follow up Clear every tracker reminder, same day
  3. Reach out One outreach touch (from Module 2)
  4. Log it If it is not tracked, it did not happen

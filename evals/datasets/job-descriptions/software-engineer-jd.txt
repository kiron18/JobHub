Senior Software Engineer — FinTech Platform
Afterpay | Sydney, NSW (Hybrid)

About the role:
We're looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our core payments platform team. You'll design and build distributed systems that process millions of transactions daily across Australia, New Zealand, and the US.

What you'll do:
- Architect and implement scalable microservices using TypeScript and Node.js
- Design and optimise PostgreSQL databases handling high transaction volumes
- Build and maintain REST and GraphQL APIs consumed by mobile and web clients
- Lead technical design discussions and mentor junior engineers
- Collaborate with Product and Data teams to define technical requirements
- Champion engineering best practices: code review, testing, documentation

What we're looking for:
- 5+ years professional software engineering experience
- Strong TypeScript and Node.js expertise
- Deep understanding of relational databases (PostgreSQL preferred)
- Experience with cloud infrastructure (AWS or GCP)
- Familiarity with containerisation (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Understanding of payment systems or financial technology is a plus
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills

Tech stack: TypeScript, Node.js, React, PostgreSQL, Redis, AWS, GitHub Actions

Why Afterpay:
Flexible working, competitive salary, equity participation, learning & development budget.

Data Analyst — Financial Services
ANZ Banking Group | Melbourne, VIC (Hybrid)

About the role:
Join ANZ's Business Intelligence team to transform complex financial data into actionable insights that drive strategic decisions across our retail banking division.

Key responsibilities:
- Develop and maintain Tableau dashboards used by 50+ stakeholders across the business
- Write complex SQL queries to extract, transform, and analyse large datasets
- Conduct ad hoc analysis to support business decisions and regulatory reporting
- Partner with business units to understand data requirements and translate them into analytical solutions
- Identify trends and anomalies in transaction data and present findings to senior leadership
- Automate recurring reports using Python (pandas, numpy)

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in a data analysis or business intelligence role
- Advanced SQL skills — comfortable with window functions, CTEs, complex joins
- Proficiency in Tableau or similar BI tools (Power BI, Looker)
- Python experience for data manipulation (pandas, numpy)
- Strong stakeholder management and presentation skills
- Experience in financial services or banking preferred
- Tertiary qualification in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or related field

What we offer:
Competitive salary, annual performance bonus, flexible working arrangements, career development pathways.

**Used for:** Section intro carousels and contextual "here's what this page does" banners.

## Slots

| Slot | Type | Example | Notes |
|------|------|---------|-------|
| `{{BANNER_TEXT}}` | string | "70% of Aussie roles are filled via networking. This is your LinkedIn toolkit..." | Body text. Keep under ~200 characters for layout fit. |

## Visual notes

- Alt-fill background (`#F4EFE8`) with whisper border and 16px radius.
- Petrol (`#2D5A6E`) left border on the text serves as the required accent element.
- X close button is decorative only (non-interactive mock).
- Height expands with text content; ~84px estimate is for 2-3 lines.

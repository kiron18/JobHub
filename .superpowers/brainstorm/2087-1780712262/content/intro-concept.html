The Six Second Resume Scan — Visual Concept

After CV scan completes, user sees their resume being "viscerally judged"

Here's the mental model:

The user just uploaded their resume. The scan ran. Now instead of just showing a text report, we show what a hiring manager sees — their resume flipping past, being evaluated in those critical 6 seconds.

Proposed sequence:

  1. Resume 1 appears — squiggly paper effect, handwritten-style countdown starts at 00:06
  2. Countdown ticks down to 00:00 with the squiggly turbulence effect
  3. At 00:00 — resume flies off-screen with Disney anticipation (leans back first, then whips off)
  4. Resume 2 slides in — overlapping action, countdown resets to 00:06
  5. Repeat for Resume 3
  6. After all 3 — transition into the actual report ("What's costing you callbacks")

Engineer Prompt: On-Page Job Search Diagnostic & Conversion Flow

Objective: To deliver a highly personalized job search diagnostic directly on the page, immediately followed by clear, low-friction pathways to engage with our core AI platform and, secondarily, our free community. The page must maximize immediate conversion to the AI platform's free generations.

Page Flow & Elements:

1
Dynamic Page Title/Header:
•
Content: "Your Personalized Job Search Diagnostic, [User's First Name]!"
•
Functionality: Pulls user's first name from the initial lead capture form.
2
Introduction Section:
•
Content: A personalized message acknowledging their unique challenges as an international graduate/skilled migrant in Australia. Articulate their core problem clearly, setting the stage for the diagnostic.
•
Example: "Hi [User's First Name], we've analyzed your situation and understand the unique hurdles you're facing – from visa filters to unfamiliar CV standards. This diagnostic will pinpoint exactly what's holding you back and show you the path forward."
3
Diagnostic Results (6 Sections):
•
Structure: Present the diagnostic in 6 distinct sections (e.g., Targeting, Document Audit, Pipeline, The Honest Truth, Your 3-Step Fix, What JobHub Does For You).
•
Content per Section:
•
Problem Identification: Clearly articulate a specific problem relevant to the section.
•
Solution/Strategy: Replace "fix" with a stronger term like "Your Path Forward," "Strategic Solution," or "Recommended Approach." This should hint at the solution without fully revealing the "how" (which is your platform).
•
Personalized Message (Optional, before "The Honest Truth"): Include another short, personalized message here to maintain engagement.
•
Example: "Before we get to the core, [User's First Name], let's address the elephant in the room..."
4
"The Honest Truth" Section:
•
Content: This section should articulate the single biggest leverage point or mistake they're likely making. It should create a compelling "gap" that your platform fills.
•
Example: "The #1 reason international graduates get stuck isn't a lack of skill; it's a positioning problem. You're trying to fit into a system not built for you, instead of leveraging a system designed to highlight your unique value."
5
On-Page Social Proof Collection (After "The Honest Truth" or "Your 3-Step Fix"):
•
Placement: Prominently displayed, but not interrupting the core flow.
•
Mechanism:
•
Headline: "How accurate was your diagnostic?"
•
Rating: 5-star rating system (clickable).
•
Predefined Feedback: A few clickable options for common sentiments (e.g., "Spot on!", "Really insightful," "Helped me understand my problem," "Needs more detail").
•
Optional Text Box: A small, optional text area for free-form comments.
•
Optional Name Field: "Share your name (optional, for anonymous sharing)."
•
Functionality: Submits data to your CRM/database for testimonial collection.
6
"Your 3-Step Fix" Section (Introducing the AI Platform):
•
Content: Position your AI platform as the definitive, easy-to-implement solution to the problems just diagnosed.
•
Headline: "Your Personalized 3-Step System to Turn This Diagnosis into a Job Offer:"
•
Bullet Points: Outline 3 key benefits of the AI platform, directly addressing the diagnostic points (e.g., "AI-Powered Document Optimization," "Targeted Outreach & Unadvertised Roles," "Interview Prep & Confidence").
•
Reinforce Value: Briefly explain *why* your platform is the unfair advantage (speed, scale, objectivity).
•
Example: "Kiron, the hard strategic thinking is done. You have real results, genuine skills, and a story that works, it just needs to be told at the right altitude for graduate roles. Our AI platform helps you build the two things you need most right now: ATS-optimized resume variations that match the exact language of the roles you're targeting, and tailored cover letter frameworks that connect your experience to each specific employer. You're not starting from scratch, you're refining a strong foundation. The silence you've been experiencing isn't a reflection of your capability; it's a positioning problem, and positioning is fixable. Let's get you in front of the right people, telling the right story, so they can see what we already see: someone ready to contribute from day one."
7
7
Primary Call to Action (CTA) - AI Platform Free Generations:
•
Placement: Immediately following "Your 3-Step Fix" section. This must be the most prominent CTA on the page.
•
Button Text: "Start Your Job Search Fix Now – Get 5 Free AI Generations!"
•
Functionality:
•
Clicking this button should *immediately* grant the user access to 5 free generations within the AI platform.
•
Crucial: This should happen seamlessly without requiring a separate sign-up form or credit card input *at this stage*. The user is already identified from the diagnostic lead capture. The free generations are a direct, frictionless entry into the product experience.
•
Goal: Get them *using* the product instantly.
8
Secondary Call to Action (CTA) - Free Skool Community:
•
Placement: Visually distinct, but immediately below the primary CTA. It should be less prominent than the primary CTA.
•
Button Text: "Need More Support? Join Our Free Community"
•
Content (brief text above button): "Alongside our powerful AI platform, connect with 2,847 international graduates in our free Skool community. Get weekly video resources, templates, and live Q&A calls."
•
Functionality: Links directly to your Skool community.
9
Exit-Intent Pop-up (for users attempting to leave the page):
•
Trigger: When a user's mouse moves towards closing the tab or navigating away.
•
Headline: "Wait! Don't leave without your unfair advantage."
•
Body Text: "You've just seen your diagnostic. Now, get 5 FREE AI-powered document generations to fix your job search *today*. No credit card needed, no sign-up form – you're already in. Just click below to instantly access your free tools."
•
Button Text: "Claim My 5 Free AI Generations Now!"
•
Functionality: Same as the primary CTA – grants immediate access to 5 free generations within the AI platform without further credential input.
General Page Requirements:

•
Mobile Responsiveness: Ensure the entire page and all elements are fully optimized for mobile devices.
•
Speed: Page load times must be minimal to prevent abandonment.
•
Tracking: Implement analytics (e.g., Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel) to track page views, scroll depth, CTA clicks, and pop-up interactions.
•
Personalization: Ensure all instances of `[User's First Name]` are dynamically populated.
This comprehensive plan ensures the diagnostic is a powerful, integrated tool for both delivering value and driving conversions to your core offering.

👋 **Who are we**

Moodi is one of Australasia’s largest and fastest-growing wellness brands.

We create mental wellness supplements that pair science-backed ingredients with delicious flavours to support gut health, stress, sleep, and more.

Since launching in 2022, we’ve become a large e-commerce business with 220,000+ online customers and we consistently sit in the top 1% of spenders on Meta globally.

We are also the #1 wellness drinks brand and have one of the fastest-selling protein snacks in NZ supermarkets.

Our growth has been fuelled by a combination of marketing innovation and operational excellence across multiple product ranges, channels and geographies.

Moodi is powered by a lean, high-performing team with big ambitions. If you’re looking to join a rapidly scaling, purpose-driven brand (and you’re not afraid of hard work!) read on and apply.

* * *

✨ **The role**

The Head of Growth at Moodi is a key leadership role charged with scaling our performance marketing through the next phase of rapid growth. This role is critical in building our performance marketing capabilities within our DTC channel in new and existing geographies. You will be responsible for driving profitable growth through paid social, e-commerce and retention.

This is both a strategic and hands-on role, suited to a leader who can design, build and optimise scalable digital marketing strategies, implement rigorous testing processes that drive incremental performance improvements, and inspire a high-performance culture. The ideal candidate brings deep experience developing world-class performance marketing strategies in fast-growing DTC consumer brands. You thrive in high-growth environments, make data-driven decisions, and are motivated by the opportunity to profitably scale our marketing efforts for long-term, international success.

* * *

🤳 **What you’ll do**

**Growth strategy**

- **Growth strategy development:** Develop and lead Moodi’s growth strategy across paid social, website, and retention, aligned with broader business goals.

- **Customer insights:** Collect and analyse customer/user insights to inform data-driven growth strategies.

- **Marketing localisation:** Develop and own localisation of growth strategy to ensure initiatives resonate and perform in each region.


**Paid social**

- **Paid acquisition strategy**: Own Moodi’s paid social strategy and execution through the entire funnel across Meta, TikTok, and emerging channels, relentlessly focusing on maximising total contribution margin and reaching new audiences at the lowest cost.

- **Paid testing:** Design and implement structured experiments and testing frameworks to improve performance, validate hypotheses and drive incrementality across creative, marketing mix and customer touchpoints.

- **Paid measurement:** Own attribution strategy and marketing effectiveness measurement, implementing frameworks like holdout testing, Marketing Mix Modelling (MMM) and incrementality to inform decision-making.

- **Media buying and budget management:** Oversee media buying, including campaign structures, testing frameworks and budget allocation to maximise marketing efficiency and support rapid experimentation.


**Creative strategy**

- **Creative direction:** Lead the creative direction for paid media, ensuring content is aligned with performance insights and brand messaging.

- **Creative alignment:** Ensure creative and campaign execution across channels aligns with brand values, positioning, and builds long-term brand equity, while delivering measurable performance outcomes.

- **Creative targets:** Set and manage creative volume and diversity targets across the creative pillars and marketing calendar in line with spend forecasts.

- **Creative operations:** Implement and improve scalable systems to systematically test and optimise ad creatives whilst driving down the cost per creative.

- **Creative partnerships:** Lead the commercial structure and direction of paid partnerships with prominent creators/influencers to drive a measured ROI.

- **Vendor management:** Identify and partner with external creative agencies or contractors to supplement creative needs, where appropriate.

- **Creator partnerships:** Lead commercial partnerships and strategies with creators, influencers, ambassadors and/or affiliates that drive business and brand performance.


**Other channels**

- **Out of home (OOH)**: Develop and execute on Moodi’s OOH strategy as part of top-of-funnel efforts across DTC and retail.

- **Other channels:** Test and validate other channels, including podcasts, radio, CTV, direct mail, PR, affiliate etc.


**Website & CRO**

- **CRO management:** Implement and manage CRO frameworks and tools to run consistent testing and deliver measurable improvements.

- **Landing page and site development**: Lead the development of landing pages, on-site assets and localised storefronts in collaboration with internal and external partners.

- **SEO and discoverability**: Oversee SEO performance and optimisation, ensuring Moodi content is competitive and well-indexed, including in AI models.


**E-commerce operations**

- **Marketing launches:** Own website updates and execution during marketing moments (e.g., product launches).

- **Tech stack ownership**: Own and optimise e-commerce platform tools and integrations, ensuring cross-functional alignment with CX and Ops. Unlocking efficiency and insights through the use of AI-enabled workflows and tools.

- **New store builds**: Lead the planning and execution of major site updates (e.g., re-theming) and adding international instances as Moodi expands into new markets.


**Retention**

- **CRM and lifecycle ownership**: Own Moodi’s email, SMS and CRM strategy - optimising customer journeys with data-driven segmentation to improve LTV and repeat rate.

- **New retention levers**: Identify and execute retention tactics beyond email, such as loyalty, gifting, product drops and community activation, to further improve LTV and repeat rate.


**Financial management & profit optimisation**

- **Financial discipline:** Set and own performance targets to ensure first-order profitability and maximise total contribution margin across each product range and geography.

- **Budgeting and forecasting**: Partner with leadership to own the growth budget, forecast marketing performance, and optimise return on spend.

- **Incrementality measurement:** Develop frameworks and testing methodologies to ensure incrementality of all new and existing channels.


**Leadership**

- **Team & function leadership:** Build, develop and lead a world-class growth function and team (both internal and external), ensuring alignment with Moodi’s culture, values, and growth objectives.

- **Resource planning:** Plan, schedule, and allocate resources effectively to ensure growth objectives and targets are met.

- **Business partnering:** Act as a strategic growth partner across the business, collaborating cross-functionally to align growth with company strategy, customer insights, and long-term **brand equity.**

- **Data Protection:** Collaborate with leadership to manage third-party risks, data privacy obligations, and regulatory compliance (e.g., Australian and NZ Privacy Acts, GDPR, SOC 2).

- **Collaborative leadership:** Foster a proactive, collaborative leadership approach, creating a high-performance culture that drives success across teams and functions.
